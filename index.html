About Me

Style with a Smile,
Stay a While.

Hey there! I'm Rivibibu Prabashwara

I am currently a student at Richmond College , I am 17 years old. My journey begins in a small village in the south of Sri Lanka, which I feel has reached its peak by now.

I am passionate about exploring the mysteries of our world and beyond. Anthropology and astrophysics captured by my curiosity, guiding me on a quest to understand the universe and the human story as it unfolds across time and cultures. Other than that I pursue digital coding and software development as a hobby. I can assure you that using computers and coding has turned a new page in my life. It is a way to develop my creative ideas.

My journey that started from a small village has come a long way. I try want to remember well everyone who was in the middle of it. And I thank them.
